Dataset: mtcars

In observing patterns in the charts, the histogram shows a shift in MPG among cars with different cylinder counts. It shows a distinct pattern: more cylinders are associated with lower MPG. This presents a clear negative relationship between cylinder count and MPG.

I aligned my design with Few and Yau by using color and labels to distinguish metrics. For example, the dashed line in my histogram represents the mean and provides a clear reference.

I disagree with some of Few’s recommendations. Few emphasizes strictly data‑driven visuals where color and decoration always encode meaning. I think limited, tasteful decoration and color without strict statistical encoding can help users analyze the data. In my opinion, such elements can aid interpretation when used sparingly.
